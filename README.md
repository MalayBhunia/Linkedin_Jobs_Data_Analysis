## üìå Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive exploratory data analysis (EDA) of job postings on LinkedIn within the Indian market. By analyzing approximately 950 job listings, the project uncovers trends in job roles, experience levels, industry sectors, and geographical distribution of opportunities in India.

## üìÅ Data Source
The dataset used for this analysis is a collection of LinkedIn job postings specifically focused on the Indian job market.

- **Source:** The dataset was sourced from ` Kaggle.`.
- File link: [https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/michellemiranda/linkedin-jobs-dataset-for-india].
- **Dataset Size:** The dataset contains **949 individual job listings** with **15 distinct features**.
- **Data Collection Period:** Based on the `publishedAt` field, the job listings represent a snapshot of the market as of **January 2024**.
- **Key Attributes:**
    - `id`: Unique identifier for the job post.
    - `publishedAt`: The date the job was posted.
    - `title`: Original job designation (later normalized in this project).
    - `companyName`: Name of the hiring organization.
    - `applicationsCount`: The number of candidates who applied for the role.
    - `experienceLevel`: Required experience (e.g., Entry Level, Mid-Senior).
    - `sector`: The industry category of the hiring company.
    - `city` & `state`: Geographical location of the job within India.



## üîç Key Insights from Analysis
1. **Top Roles:** Identified the top 10 most in-demand job titles (e.g., Data Scientist, HR, Analyst).
2. **Experience Demand:** Mid-Senior level positions show the highest demand across the top 6 hiring sectors.
3. **Geographical Hubs:** Visualized job density across different Indian states and cities.
4. **Sector Trends:** Breakdown of hiring across IT, Staffing, Manufacturing, and more.
5. **Application Trends:** Analysis of which roles attract the most competition.

## üìà Visualizations Included
- Treemaps for Sector distribution.
- Categorical plots for Experience-Level demand.
- Bar charts for Top Hiring Companies and Job Titles.
- Geographic heatmaps of job availability.
